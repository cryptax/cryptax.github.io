# Auvergn'hack 2026

Auvergn'hack is a recent, local security conference in Clermont Ferrand. It is rather small: a single day and a single track, but it was a fabulous discovery with an **excellent ratio of interesting talks**.

Yes, when you're a "senior" with "20+ years of experience" (a kinder way of saying you're old), you've already seen quite a few new technologies and talks. You don't say it too often not to sound old and grumpy, but you are often disappointed with talks because they tell a story so similar to one you've already heard in the past. You end up finding more value in *networking*. Actually, this is fine, and another reason for joing conferences.

At Auvergn'hack, **on the 4 conferences I followed in the afternoon, 3 were deeply interesting**. This is the *best ratio I've had at conferences in ages*. Hey, only 4 conferences, you're wondering? Well, in the morning, I was busy with my own workshop and couldn't attend any. And in the afternoon, I only missed one (needed to purchase a toothbrush lol). Auvergn'hack is not one of those enormous, multi-track conferences.
